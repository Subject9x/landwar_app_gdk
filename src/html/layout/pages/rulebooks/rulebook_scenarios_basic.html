LANDWAR

Basic Scenarios

Contained here are some rules for playing basic scenarios of LANDWAR regardless of the armies involved. Each Scenario is built of a standard template that indicates how long the scenario should last, force size, win conditions, etc.

They are built this way to help you make custom scenarios more easily, and to quickly refer to any information during game play.

Scenario template

Definitions of some common terms and ideas for all scenarios in this document.

For specific rules, go to section after the scenario list below.

  • Game Length: How many turns, or no limit.
  • Board Size: Game is balanced around a 3'x3' square.
  • Army Size: suggested force size for players.
  • Win Condition: How to determine the scenario winner.
  • Deployment Style: how Player Armies are placed onto the play area, shape of area, etc.
  • Board Edge: type of board edge rules to be used.

Contact Battle

Two opposing armies have crashed into each other in this sector. Each force aims to dominate the sector in preparation for larger operations. Victory goes to the side left standing or on the field of battle!

  • Game Length: both players choose 8, 10, or 12 turns, or no limit.
  • Board Size: recommended Standard Size of 3' x 3'
  • Army Size: players choose, a good starter is 1000pts or 4-6 models.
  • Win Condition: Standard Attrition.
  • Deployment Stye: Standard.

Setup

  • Players mark out the 3'x3' playing area.
  • Players build their army lists.
  • Add terrain as desired to the play area.
    • Make sure to agree on the properties as seen under Terrain Rules(Core rulebook).
  • Players then determine who will setup their forces first, the player that does receives a +2 INI to their Initiative roll on Turn 1.
  • Each Player sets up their entire army opposite the other player, using the entire length of their board edge.
    • Players may deploy units no more than 6" from their Board Edge.
  • Once setup is complete, set a marker for turn-counting and begin the game!

Defensive Line

One Army has been building up its forces, biding its time for the offensive...

the other has been constructing and preparing defenses, hoping to stop the incursion at the first line of defense.

  • Game Length: 10 turns or player choice.
  • Board Size: recommended Standard Size of 3' x 3'
  • Army Size: players choose, a good starter is 1000pts or 4-6 models.
  • Win Condition: Local Objectives.
  • Deployment Stye: Standard Offensive.

Setup

  • First players decide who is the Attacker, and who is the Defender.
  • Players mark out the 3'x3' playing area.
  • Players build their army lists.
  • Add terrain as desired to the play area.
    • Make sure to agree on the properties as seen under Terrain Rules(Core rulebook).
  • Players then determine who will setup their forces first, the player that does receives a +2 INI to their Initiative roll on Turn 1.
  • Each Player sets up their entire army opposite the other player, using the entire length of their board edge.
    • Players may deploy units no more than 6" from their Board Edge.
  • Once setup is complete, set a marker for turn-counting and begin the game!

Win Condition types

  • Standard Attrition
  • Local Objectives
  • Forced Withdrawal
  • Headhunters
